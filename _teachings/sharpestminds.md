## Mentorship Focus

As a **SharpestMinds mentor**, I work with early-career ML engineers to bridge the gap between academic training and industry expectations.

### Areas of Guidance

- **Production ML skills** — what companies actually use vs. what bootcamps teach
- **Portfolio projects** — how to build ML projects that demonstrate real engineering judgment
- **Interview preparation** — ML system design, coding, and behavioral interviews
- **Career strategy** — target company selection, positioning, and networking

### Mentee Outcomes

Mentees have successfully transitioned into ML engineering roles at startups and established tech companies, with a focus on applied ML and MLOps positions.
