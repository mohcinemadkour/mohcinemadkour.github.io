## Course Overview

Served as lead instructor for an intensive **AI/ML Bootcamp** training working professionals for careers in machine learning and AI engineering.

### Topics Covered

- **Machine Learning Foundations** — supervised/unsupervised learning, model evaluation, feature engineering
- **Deep Learning** — neural networks, CNNs, RNNs with PyTorch
- **NLP & Text** — text classification, embeddings, transformers fundamentals
- **Production ML** — model deployment, API wrapping with Flask/FastAPI, Docker basics
- **Projects** — each cohort completed capstone projects on real datasets

### Teaching Approach

Emphasis on **hands-on coding** from day one, real-world datasets, and production-mindset from the start. Students left with deployable projects and portfolio-ready code.
